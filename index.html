About us

Welcome to Delote-Beauty – a place where beauty and comfort meet. Our salon is a space where every client feels special and receives top-notch service. We offer a wide range of services, including haircuts, styling, manicure, pedicure, eyelash extensions, makeup, and much more.

At Delote-Beauty, we take pride in our team of experienced professionals who use only the latest technologies and high-quality materials. We keep up with the latest trends in the beauty industry to offer our clients the best and most current services.

Our specialists will not only give you a fresh look but will also make the self-care process a true pleasure. We care about each client, creating an atmosphere of comfort and harmony where you can relax and forget about the everyday hustle.

Come to Delote-Beauty and experience the difference. We are confident that every visit will be a true celebration of beauty.
